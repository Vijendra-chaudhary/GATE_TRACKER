## 1. Introduction
GATE Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

**By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal identification information (such as your name, address, or phone number) on our servers. All user data generated within the App (such as study progress, notes, and preferences) is stored locally on your device or in your personal cloud storage (Google Drive).

### B. Google Drive Data (Backup & Restore)
The App uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive API services to provide a generic backup and restore functionality.
*   **Permissions:** We request access to your Google Drive specifically to create and manage a specific folder and file for the App's data (`drive.file` scope).
*   **Usage:** This access is used **solely** to upload a backup of your local app database to your Google Drive and to retrieve it when you request a restore.
*   **Storage:** Your backup data resides in **your** Google Drive account. We do not have access to your other files, and we do not transmit your data to any third-party servers.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use the information solely to provide the App's functionality:
*   **Local Data:** To track your GATE exam preparation progress and stats.
*   **Cloud Data:** To allow you to sync your progress across devices or restore it after a reinstall.
